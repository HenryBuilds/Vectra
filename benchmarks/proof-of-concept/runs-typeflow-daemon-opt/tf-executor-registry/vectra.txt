Note: the actual `WorkflowExecutor` in `server/services/workflow-executor.ts` doesn't yet consume this registry — it dispatches via a long `if (currentNode.type === "...")` chain (workflow-executor.ts:233+). The `ExecutorRegistry` appears to be the intended target of an in-progress refactor away from that chain.
