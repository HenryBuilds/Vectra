**Short answer:**

- **Where:** `lib/encryption.ts` is the only encryption module. Credential `config` is encrypted by `encryptObject(...)` in `server/routers/credentials.ts:150-151` (create) and `:216-217` (update); decrypted in `server/services/credential-service.ts:309` and `server/routers/credentials.ts:124-125`. Stored in Postgres column `credentials.config` (jsonb) per `db/schema/credentials.ts:5-15`.
- **Algorithm:** **AES-256-GCM** (`lib/encryption.ts:16`). 16-byte random IV per record, 16-byte auth tag, serialized as `iv:authTag:ciphertext` hex.
- **Key source:** Env var **`ENCRYPTION_KEY`**, required by `lib/env.ts:23-25`. In `getEncryptionKey()` at `lib/encryption.ts:5-14`: if the value is 64 hex chars it's used directly as the 32-byte key; otherwise it's **SHA-256 hashed** to 32 bytes. No PBKDF2/scrypt/argon2 and no salt — strength relies on the entropy of `ENCRYPTION_KEY` itself.
